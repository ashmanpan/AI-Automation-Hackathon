# Participant User Guide

Welcome to the Hackathon Platform! This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of your hackathon experience. 

## Motivation

The goal of the hackathon is to allow you to gain a better understanding of how AI can be integrated in order to quickly generate workflows for Cisco Crosswork Workflow Manager, improving development agility. This is not a perfect solution and still needs work in order to be completely foolproof, but it should provide a general proof-of-concept.

## Getting Started

### Accessing your dCloud Session
1. Navigate to the following page: https://expo.ciscodcloud.com/2uqjv4ftmdxsk2pd5rrqu1f6q (Chrome recommended)
2. Click on Explore.
3. Enter your email address.
4. Click to accept the dCloud Terms & Conditions, and click Continue to receive your session.
5. Click Launch to jump into your session.
6. Access the workstation using WebRDP option.

### Dashboard Overview
Your dashboard shows:
- **Team Information**: Your team name and current rank
- **Exercise Progress**: Number of completed exercises out of total available
- **Pending Submissions**: Submissions awaiting grading
- **Total Score**: Your team's cumulative score

## Exercises

### Viewing Exercises
1. Click on "Exercises" in the sidebar
2. You'll see all exercises assigned to your team
3. Active exercises are available for submission
4. Click on an exercise to view details

### Submitting Work
1. Open an exercise from the exercises list
2. Choose your submission type:
   - **File Upload**: Submit code, documents, or other files
   - **Text Submission**: Submit text-based solutions
   - **URL Submission**: Submit links to deployed applications or repositories
3. Add an optional description explaining your submission
4. Click "Submit" to submit your work

### Time Limits
- Some exercises have time limits
- Timer starts when you first open the exercise
- Make sure to submit before the time runs out

## Submissions

### Tracking Your Submissions
1. Click on "My Submissions" in the sidebar
2. View all submissions made by your team
3. Check grading status:
   - **Pending**: Awaiting judge review
   - **Graded**: Reviewed and scored

### Viewing Grades
- Once graded, you'll see your score and feedback
- Judges may provide comments on strengths and areas for improvement

## Leaderboard

### Checking Rankings
- View the public leaderboard to see how your team ranks
- Rankings are updated in real-time as submissions are graded
- Scores are based on total points earned across all exercises

## Tips for Success

1. **Communicate with Your Team**: Coordinate on who submits what
2. **Read Instructions Carefully**: Make sure you understand the requirements
3. **Submit Early**: Don't wait until the last minute
4. **Quality Over Quantity**: Focus on doing exercises well rather than rushing
5. **Ask Questions**: If something is unclear, reach out to organizers

## Support

If you encounter any issues or have questions:
- Contact the hackathon organizers
- Check for announcements on the dashboard
- Reach out to judges during designated help hours

Good luck and happy hacking!
